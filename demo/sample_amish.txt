Ada Mae (Yoder) Miller, age 74 years, 3 months and 12 days, of Jamesport, Missouri, passed away peacefully at her home on Wednesday, February 19, 2026, surrounded by her family. Ada was born November 7, 1951, in Holmes County, Ohio, the daughter of Eli J. and Fannie (Troyer) Yoder. On November 15, 1970, she was united in marriage to Amos J. Miller in Holmes County. They moved to the Jamesport community in 1975, where they have resided since. Ada was a devoted member of the Old Order Amish church. She was known throughout the community for her beautiful quilting, her abundant garden, and her generous spirit. She loved hosting church services in her home and could always be counted on to help a neighbor in need. Ada is survived by her husband, Amos Miller of the home; eight children, Mary Schwartz and husband Daniel of Jamesport, Eli Miller and wife Katie of Jamesport, Fannie Borntrager and husband John of Jamesport, Samuel Miller and wife Ada of Jamesport, Anna Yoder and husband Levi of Seymour, David Miller and wife Sarah of Jamesport, Emma Hershberger and husband Abe of Bremen, Indiana, and Martha Miller of the home; forty-two grandchildren; eleven great-grandchildren; four sisters, Fannie Troyer of Jamesport, Mary Hershberger of Jamesport, Emma Yoder of Holmes County, Ohio, and Katie Schrock of Arthur, Illinois; and two brothers, John Yoder of Jamesport and Daniel Yoder of Jamesport. She was preceded in death by her parents; a son, Jacob Miller, in infancy; a daughter-in-law, Rebecca Miller; a brother, Amos Yoder; and a sister, Sarah Bontrager. Funeral services were held Saturday, February 22, 2026, at the family home. Burial followed in the Amish Community Cemetery near Jamesport.
